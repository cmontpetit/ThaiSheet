Recordings under review

These synthesized recordings are provisional, not definitive pronunciation references. Isolated Thai characters and vowel forms can be particularly difficult for text-to-speech voices to pronounce correctly.

The longer-term goal is to produce a reviewed recording set with a native Thai speaker. Until then, please use the report links below to flag anything that sounds wrong.

The app bundles three recorded voices — the default is ElevenLabs Matilda, alongside Google Neural2-C and Chirp3-HD Kore. Choose a voice below to preview and compare the complete recording sets.
