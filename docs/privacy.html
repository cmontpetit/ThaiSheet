Last updated: July 10, 2026

ThaiSheet is designed as an offline-first learning app. It does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party iOS SDKs.

Data Collection

ThaiSheet does not collect personal data and does not send learning activity to the developer.

The app stores the following data on your device:

  • Flashcard progress, including SRS stage, review counts, last review date, and next review date.
  • App settings, including filters, learning strategy, language preference, and whether iCloud Sync is enabled.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync, ThaiSheet syncs learning progress and settings through Apple's iCloud key-value store so your progress can appear on your other devices signed into the same Apple ID.

The developer does not operate a server for this data and cannot access your iCloud account.

Tracking and Advertising

ThaiSheet does not track users across apps or websites. ThaiSheet does not show ads.

For support or privacy questions, see the support page.
